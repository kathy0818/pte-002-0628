1. FIB #386 Product Selling
查看原题
AI 答案解析

Once an organization has its product to sell, it must then determine the appropriate price to sell it at. The price is set by  balancing (答案: balancing)  many factors including supply-and-demand, cost, desired profit competition, perceived value, and market behavior. Ultimately, the final price is  determined (答案: determined)  by what the market is willing to  exchange (答案: exchange)  for the product. Pricing theory can be quite complex because so many factors influence what the purchaser decides is a fair  value (答案: value)  .

It also should be  pointed (答案: noted)  that, in addition to monetary exchange, price can be the exchange of goods or services as in a barter agreement, or an exchange of specific behavior, such as a vote in a political campaign.

Choices :
1.comparing,begetting,balancing,offsetting
2.collected,mitigated,determined,exhausted
3.consign,design,exchange,prepare
4.addition,shape,content,value
5.pointed,enlarged,overrated,noted
解析 :
1.balancing

【思路】The price is set by ____ many factors including…本句大意为：产品价格是由 包括……在内的诸多因素所决定（set by）的。备选项中，首先排除 begetting “引起、导致”和 offsetting “弥补、抵消”，均不搭配宾语“因素”。再看 comparing “对比、比较”和 balancing“平衡”，结合生活常识，商品的价格是多种因素共同作用的结果，是在综合考虑之后做出的，而不是对比多种因素，所以排除comparing，选择 balancing “平衡、均衡”，表示“平衡多种因素”。

【翻译】价格是通过平衡许多因素来确定的，包括……

【考点】单句理解+单词辨析

2.determined

【思路】... the final price is ____ by what the market is willing to ... for the product … 本句大意为：产品的最终价格是由市场愿意为该产品 ... 。备选项中，排除 collected “收集”，mitigated “减轻”和 exhausted “耗尽”，因为代入后明显都说不通。只有 determined “决定”和 price “价格”是搭配的，所以应该选择 determined。

【翻译】产品的最终价格是由市场愿意为该产品 ... 决定的。

【考点】单句理解+单词辨析

3.exchange

【思路】Ultimately, the final price is determined by what the market is willing to ____ for the product. 前文列举了影响定价的诸多因素，本句更进一步，提到最终决定价格的因素—市场。what从句意为：市场愿意用什么东西____该产品。观察备选项，其中 consign “寄存、委托”、design “设计”、prepare “准备”、 exchange “交换”。再根据在市场中的交易行为就是以物换物，或以钱换物，因此只能选择 exchange。

【翻译】归根结底，最终价格是由市场愿意为产品交换什么来决定的。

【考点】单句理解+词意辨析

4.value

【思路】Pricing theory can be quite complex because so many factors influence what the purchaser decides is a fair ____ . 该句大意为：因为很多因素都会影响购买者认为是公平的 ，所以价格理论可能会很复杂。本句再次重复了定价的过程：各种因素对定价的影响。而句中已经出现了“因素”，可知空格处应填入表达“定价”的名词。观察备选项，addition “增添物”、shape “形状”、 content “内容”和 value “价值，价格”，可知应选择 value 。

【翻译】定价理论可能相当复杂，因为有太多的因素影响着购买者决定的公允价值。

【考点】上下文理解+单词辨析

5.noted

【思路】It also should be ____ that, ... , price can be the exchange of goods or services as in a barter agreement ... , 选项中，pointed “被指”，没有引导 that 从句的用法；enlarged “增大”，overrated “高估”，容易判断都说不通；只有 noted “注意”，是合适的选项。

【翻译】另外应该注意到，... ，价格能成为货物或服务的交换，正如在物物交换协议中那样 ... 。

【考点】单句理解+单词辨析

你的答案 :
balancing, determined, exchange, value, pointed

2. FIB #387 Green Spaces
查看原题
AI 答案解析

Green spaces contribute significantly to a  reduction (答案: reduction)  in soil and aerial temperatures during spells of hot weather, so contributing to human wellbeing. In the garden  context (答案: context)  , there is, however, little information as to what extent various types of plants  defer (答案: differ)  in their cooling potential and how certain planting combinations may maximize cooling under a scenario of  low (答案: low)  rainfall and minimal water inputs.

Choices :
1.genesis,conclusion,purification,reduction
2.background,level,context,volume
3.confer,differ,alternate,defer
4.total,low,parallel,dropped
解析 :
1. reduction

【思路】原句语意：绿地有利于_____土壤和空气的温度，根据后面的 so contributing to human wellbeing “对人类的健康有好处”，那么在炎热（hot weather）的天气下， 降温会对人更好，因此在选项 genesis (起源) , conclusion (总结) , purification (净化) , reduction (减少，降低) 中，只能选择 reduction (减少，降低)。

【考点】 上下文理解

【句意】在炎热的天气期间，绿地大大有助于降低土壤和空气温度，因此有助于人类福祉。

2. context

【思路】原句：In the garden____, there is, however, little information as to ...，选项中，background“背景”，in the background 是“（计算机程序）在后台；背景的”，在此处显然说不通；level“水平”，volume在“体积；音量”，也都明显说不通；只有context“环境”才合适，in the garden context 意思是“在花园环境中”。

【考点】词义辨析

【句意】然而，在园林环境中，关于不同类型的植物......

3. differ

【思路】there is, however, little information as to what extent various types of plants_____ in their cooling potential. 首先分析句子结构： as 做连词，连接了前后两个句子。主语为 various types of plants , 空格为该句的谓语动词。根据常识，可以判断“不同的植物的冷却潜力”是不同的，所以在选项 confer (商讨，授予) , differ (不同于) , alternate (交替；轮流), defer (推迟) 中，只有 differ 能表达不同。

【考点】 句意理解

【句意】然而，在园林环境中，关于不同类型的植物在冷却潜力方面有多大差异.....

4. low

【思路】how certain planting combinations may maximize cooling under a scenario of ____rainfall and minimal water inputs.在A scenario of（场景）后面，and 连接了两个名词词组，一个是 minimal water inputs (最少的水输入)，由此推断空格处要和 minimal 对应，表示“少的，低的”，因此在 total (全部的)， low (低的) ， parallel (平行的)， dropped (动词落下、减少的过去分词)中选择 low ，搭配 rainfall，意思是“最少的降雨量”。

【考点】 并列结构

【句意】 ...以及在低降雨量和最少水输入的情况下，哪些植物组合能如何最大限度地冷却温度，人们对这方面的信息知之甚少。

你的答案 :
reduction, context, defer, low

3. FIB #28 Guide Stick
查看原题
AI 答案解析

Foldable white canes help the  insensitivity (答案: visually)  impaired navigate their surroundings. But the guide stick's tactile nature offers only so much information. The cane's user must manually find and avoid obstructions. But new high-tech canes are on the horizon.

Last year researchers in India tried to fill in some of the missing info with their experimental SmartCane. The device uses an attached ultrasonic transmitter and a sensor that vibrates the cane to warn its users when an obstacle is within three meters.

Students at the U.K.'s Birmingham City University are developing a cane that can even identify acquaintances as they  approach (答案: approach)  . Called the 'XploR' mobility cane, it includes an  skinhead (答案: embedded)  digital camera that analyzes the faces of people walking by and compares their images against a database stored on a memory card in the cane's handle.

If there's a facial recognition match, the cane alerts the user's  smartphone (答案: smartphone)  via Bluetooth. The phone then identifies the approaching person to the user via its speaker or earbuds. The  students (答案: students)  are building a prototype they'll test later this year.

The hurdles are significant: facial recognition is a tough problem, especially outdoors. But if the XploR works, it could actually give the visually impaired a leg up on everyone else — especially those of us who never remember people's names.

Choices :
1.felicity,insensitivity,visually,malleability
2.likelihood,throat,northernmost,approach
3.untested,embedded,deadest,skinhead
4.waterborne,alone,smartphone,postpone
5.jurisprudence,bootless,students,jukebox
解析 :
1. visually

【思路】Foldable white canes help the ____ impaired navigate their surroundings. 该空考察the+形容词表示名词的结构，比如“the rich”表示“富人”，这里的 the impaired 表示健康受损的人，所以该句的主干结构完整（sth help sb do sth），选择一个副词，选项里只有visually“看得见地”与话题契合，the visually impaired 表示“视障者”。其他选项：felicity n. 幸福/得体；insensitiveness n. 不灵敏; malleability n. （金属的）可锻性，在词性和意思上均不符合。

【句意】可折叠的白色手杖帮助视力受损者在周围环境中导航。

【考点】副词；单句理解

2.approach

【思路】Students at the U.K.’s Birmingham City University are developing a cane that can even identify acquaintances as they ____，该空位于as引导的从句里，缺乏谓语，所以选择一个动词，选项里的动词有：throat“用喉音”；approach“接近”，上文提到这个 cane可以辨认熟人，在他们____时，放入 approach 更合适语境，所以选择 approach“接近”，表达在熟人走近时可以辨别熟人。

【句意】英国伯明翰城市大学的学生正在研发一种手杖，甚至可以在熟人走近时辨认他们。

【考点】从句；单句理解

3. embedded

【思路】 Called the ‘XploR’ mobility cane, it includes an ____ digital camera that analyzes the faces of people walking by and compares their images against a database stored on a memory card in the cane’s handle. 该空受 an 修饰，说明是元音开头的单词，选项有：untested“未经检验的”；embedded“嵌入的”，又因为后文提到这个 digital camera 用于分析和比较，说明是已经经过实践，排除untested，而手杖是比较小巧的东西，所以相机需要嵌入才比较方便实用，所以选择 embedded“嵌入的”。

【句意】它被称为“XploR”移动手杖，包括一个嵌入式数码相机，用于分析路过的人的面部，并将他们的图像与手杖手柄中存储在存储卡上的数据库进行比较。

【考点】形容词+名词结构；单句理解

4. smartphone

【思路】If there’s a facial recognition match, the cane alerts the user’s ____ via Bluetooth. 该空受user's修饰，表示是用户拥有的东西，且 via Bluetooth 表示通过蓝牙传输，选项有：waterborne“水运的”；alone“独自的”；smartphone“智能手机”；postpone“延期”，该空所在句子缺乏宾语，应当选择名词，且是用户拥有的东西，选择 smartphone“智能手机”。

【句意】如果有面部识别匹配，手杖通过蓝牙提醒用户的智能手机。

【考点】单句理解

5. students

【思路】The ____ are building a prototype they’ll test later this year. 该空谓语系动词 are 之前，句子缺乏复数主语，选项里只有students“学生”符合。其他选项：jurisprudence 法律学；bootless adj 无用的；jukebox 自动点唱机，均不符合。

【句意】学生们正在建造一个他们将在今年晚些时候测试的原型。

【考点】主谓一致；单句理解

你的答案 :
insensitivity, approach, skinhead, smartphone, students

4. FIB #33 Clown Fish
查看原题
AI 答案解析

Clown fish became famous thanks to the movie Finding Nemo. In real life, their social hierarchy is simple: larger fish dominate their smaller  palms (答案: counterparts)  . Now we know that to reinforce this social structure, the fish communicate with aggressive and submissive audio signals. The new info is in the journal PLoS ONE.

Researchers  recorded (答案: recorded)  clown fish calls,  capturing (答案: capturing)  this noise as one chased a smaller fish. These popping sounds function as an aggression signal. When a clown fish has been chased and wishes to submit, it shakes its head in a submissive gesture and produces clicking noises like these. The researchers  compared (答案: compared)  the aggressive and submissive calls, and found that the sound pulses in a submissive signal were shorter and more high-pitched.

Unlike many animals that use sound to draw in  exponential (答案: potential)  mates, clown fish appear to use their calls only as labels of social status. When a little fish makes submissive sounds to a larger one, neither has to invest in a physical confrontation. Which is good news for small-fry like Nemo.

Choices :
1.palms,prompts,traps,counterparts
2.unfolded,deported,recorded,dialed
3.cluttering,profiting,capturing,padlocking
4.pared,compared,guided,treaded
5.exponential,potential,nimble,ventral
解析 :
1.counterparts

【思路】原文In real life, their social hierarchy is simple: larger fish dominate their smaller ______. 本句由标点“冒号”可知冒号后的内容是对其前面内容的具体说明，而前面讲的是小丑鱼的社会等级制度(social hierarchy)：是大鱼统治小鱼。因此与larger fish相对应的小鱼，所以选counterparts“对应的事物”。palms“手掌/棕榈树”；prompts作名词时表示“提示符号”；traps“陷阱”在本句里语义都不恰当。

【句意】在现实生活中，他们的社会等级很简单:较大的鱼支配着较小的鱼。

【考点】词义辨析；单句理解

2.recorded

【思路】原文Researchers _____ clown fish calls, _____ this noise as one chased a smaller fish. 本空四个词的意思分别是：unfold“打开/揭露”；deport“驱逐出境”；record“记录”；dial“拨打电话/测量”。从要填的动词所搭配的宾语clown fish calls（小丑鱼的叫声）来看，首先可以排除deport和dial。然后unfold揭露的一般是事实或者真相。因此record更合适，即录制下来叫声。

【句意】研究人员录下了小丑鱼的叫声，xx到了一只追逐小鱼的声音。

【考点】动宾搭配

3.capturing

【思路】原文Researchers recorded clown fish calls, _____ this noise as one chased a smaller fish. 本句逗号前已经有了完整的主谓宾结构，从选项来看这里考察现在分词的用法，即现在分词作伴随状语。因此这里要表达和前面record伴随带来的一个动作，并且this noise指代的就是calls（叫声），因此capture“捕捉”来搭配this noise和前面record calls可以是伴随的动作。clutter“乱放”；profit“对...有用”；padlock“上锁”，在本句中语义和搭配都不太恰当。

【句意】研究人员录下了小丑鱼的叫声，捕捉到了一只追逐小鱼的声音。

【考点】现在分词；单句理解

4.compared

【思路】原文The researchers ____ the aggressive and submissive calls, and found that... 本空的动词与and found并列，说明这两个动作可能同时或者先后关系。因此，可以从后面found that的内容得到一些线索。后面提到the sound pulses in...were shorter and more high-pitched，这里有明显的比较级，说明应该是对比了submissive calls和aggressive calls。因此选择compare。其它选项：pare “去皮”；guide“引导”；tread“踩踏”，在这里搭配和语义表达都不太恰当。

【句意】研究人员比较了攻击性呼叫和顺从性呼叫，发现...

【考点】并列关系；动宾搭配

5.potential

【思路】原文 Unlike many animals that use sound to draw in ____ mates, clown fish appear to use their calls only as labels of social status. 本空要选择一个形容词去修饰名词mates（伴侣），而前面draw in表示“吸引”，即吸引xx的伴侣。选项：exponential“成指数的”，表示数字增减特别大；potential“潜在的”；nimble“灵活的/敏捷的”；ventral“腹部的”，所以从搭配来看，吸引潜在的伴侣意义更通顺。

【句意】 与许多动物用声音吸引潜在的配偶不同，小丑鱼似乎只把它们的叫声当作社会地位的标签。

【考点】搭配；单句理解

你的答案 :
palms, recorded, capturing, compared, exponential

5. FIB #79 Wind
查看原题
AI 答案解析

The world’s atmosphere is forever on the move. Wind is air in motion. Sometimes air moves slowly, giving a gentle breeze. At other times it moves rapidly, creating gales and hurricanes.  Gentle (答案: Gentle)  or fierce, wind always starts in the same way. As the sun moves through the sky, it heats up some parts of the sea and land more than others. The air above these  hot (答案: hot)  spots is warmed, becomes lighter than the surrounding air, and begins to rise. Elsewhere, cool air sinks, because it is  heavier (答案: heavier)  . Winds blow because air squeezed out by sinking, cold air is sucked in under rising, warm air. Winds will blow wherever there is a  diversity (答案: difference)  in air temperature and pressure, always flowing from high to low pressure. Some winds blow in one place, and have a local name - North America’s chinook and France's mistral. Others are part of a huge circulation pattern that sends winds over the  entire (答案: entire)  globe.

Choices :
1.convergence,diversity,discretion,difference
2.entire,all,total,wholesome
3.heavier,deeper,larger,colder
4.cold,hot,cool,warm
5.Gentle,Wild,Chill,Aloud
解析 :
1.Gentle

【思路】原文：___or fierce, wind always starts in the same way.该空后半句提到starts in the same way，因此句首提or并列的两个形容词在意思上相反产生对比，选项中只有gentle“温和的”与fierce“猛烈的”为反义词。排除其他选项：Wild“狂野的”；Aloud“大声的”；Chill“寒冷的”。（这里大家可以留意一下：选项中有一个单词跟其他三个单词表达的情感色彩有明显区别时，就可能是正确选项。）

【句意】无论是温和还是猛烈，风总是以同样的方式开始。

【考点】前后文联系、词义辨析


2.hot

【思路】The air above these___spots is warmed.该空后提到is warmed，说明气体被加热,气体通过热的地方才会升温。首先排除cold“冷的”；cool“凉爽的”。其他选项：hot“热的”；warm“温暖的”。但hot 温度更高，在这之上（above）的温度会是warm的，因此选择hot。

【句意】这些热点上方的空气变暖，变得比周围的空气轻，并开始上升。

【考点】前后文联系、词义辨析


3.heavier

【思路】原文：Elsewhere, cool air sinks, because it is___ .该句有明显的因果关系，前半句讲到冷空气下沉(sinks)，选项中只有heavier“重的”更直接的满足该逻辑关系，气体变重下沉，呼应前文提到气体变轻上升的过程。因此排除其他选项：deeper“更深的”；larger“更大的”；colder“更冷的”

【句意】在其他地方，冷空气下沉，因为它更重。
